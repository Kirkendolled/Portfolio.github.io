The Deed Factory

Visit Website

I proudly present one of my comprehensive web development projects - The Deed Factory. The platform is dedicated to providing specialized assistance in the world of property transfers. The primary objective was to simplify and streamline the process of handling quit claim deeds, making it accessible and user-friendly.

Key Features:

  • Clean and Intuitive Design: The website features a clear layout with a strong emphasis on readability, ensuring users can easily navigate and understand the services provided.
  • Graphics: I crafted a unique visual identity for The Deed Factory, ensuring consistency across all web pages.
  • Informational Content: I incorporated comprehensive details about the company's offerings, ensuring clients have a thorough understanding of the services they're investing in.
  • Interactive 4-step Process: The website highlights the process in a clear, step-by-step format, making it transparent and straightforward.
  • Integrated JotForm: I designed, formatted, and coded the forms using JotForm, ensuring users have a seamless experience when providing necessary details.
  • Contact & Support: I prioritized customer support by designing an easy-to-use contact section, encouraging users to reach out with any queries or concerns.
  • Tools & Technologies: JotForm integration, Custom CSS, JavaScript, HTML, Responsive Design.
